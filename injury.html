Injury Prevention & Recovery

The RICE Protocol is Dead: Introducing POLICE

Modern injury care has evolved past simple rest and ice. We use the POLICE framework to guide acute injury management and recovery.

The POLICE framework stands for **P**rotection, **O**ptimal **L**oading, **I**ce, **C**ompression, and **E**levation. Optimal Loading is the key difference, as it encourages gentle, controlled movement early on to stimulate tissue healing.

Understanding when to rest and when to start moving is critical for preventing chronic issues. Consult our full guide on early-stage tissue healing for best practices.
